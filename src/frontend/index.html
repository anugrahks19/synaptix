Why Synaptix?

Digital fragility is the silent killer of modern enterprise. Milliseconds of downtime cost millions in capital and trust.

The "Flatline" Problem

Traditional monitoring tools are passive. They scream "Fire!" but don't hold a hose. By the time an engineer wakes up to a PagerDuty alert, the stock market has crashed or the patient has destabilized.

Autonomous Workflow

01

Ingest & Vectorize

High-velocity streams (Finance ticks, ICU vitals, Server logs) are ingested via Pathway. Unstructured text is instantly vectorized for semantic understanding.

02

Detect & Reason

LangGraph agents monitor the stream. Unlike static rules, they use LLM reasoning to determine if a spike is a harmless glitch or a catastrophic failure.

03

Execute & Heal

The Agent executes a deterministic fix (e.g., injecting liquidity, restarting a pod, alerting a nurse) and verifies the system returns to homeostasis.
